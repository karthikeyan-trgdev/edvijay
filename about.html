Building Scalable Organizations with Purpose and Precision

Dr. Vijay is a doctor-turned-executive leader and entrepreneur who brings analytical rigor, operational clarity, and long-term vision to building scalable organizations. As Executive Director at Top Rock Group, a multi-vertical enterprise spanning interior fit-out, hospitality, and education, he plays a pivotal role in shaping strategy, systems, and execution across the portfolio.
Beginning his journey in interiors and design at Top Rock Interiors, Dr. Vijay developed deep expertise in operational execution and project-driven businesses. He later took on a zero-to-one challenge in education—founding Chandramari International School from the ground up. With no pre-existing brand, systems, or team, he led the institution from concept to a fully operational school, earning parent trust, building robust internal processes, and driving steady growth in admissions.
Dr. Vijay’s leadership approach mirrors his medical training: diagnosing structural problems, designing scalable systems, and aligning teams to deliver consistent performance. His work focuses on industries that serve core human needs, including education, innovation, food, and lifestyle, with an emphasis on predictability, efficiency, and sustainable growth. Currently, Dr. Vijay is focused on scaling Top Rock Group, strengthening cross-vertical operations, launching new ventures, and creating long-term value through disciplined execution and strategic leadership.
